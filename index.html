our Staff

For Yours Knowledge

Director General of Police

They are responsible for administration of all the Police departments in the State. Their main role is to supervise and check and ensure that the department is properly functioning and has disciplinary authority.

read more

Inspector General of Police

The IG looks after the overall administrative, financial and other duties of the Police force. They are only appointed from among the IPS officers. They exercise command over the Police Force and they also perform other functions delegated to them by the legislation.

read more

Deputy Inspector General

They are ranked above the Superintendent of Police. The DIG helps the IG in supervising and overseeing the various police stations under their jurisdiction.

read more

Deputy Commissioner of Police

As a DCP, one has to enforce policies and activities given by the central government or state government. They have the power to inspect and monitor the work done by other junior officials. They also act as an appellate authority in revenue.

read more

Deputy Superintendent of police

They are incharge of executing all duties and maintain law and order or any other duties as assigned by the Superintendent of Police. They also supervise the work of their juniors. They also look after all the cases registered by the other police station within their jurisdiction.

read more

Police Inspector

The job of an Inspector is to supervise the investigation under his jurisdiction. They also train junior investigating officers investigating the case under their jurisdiction. They also supervise all official works including maintenance of records and report it to senior Police officers.

read more

Sub Inspector

They ensure the effective working of the management or staff subordinate to them. Their main agenda is to maintain peace and prevention and detection of crimes. They also maintain a register in the Police station.

read more

Assistant Sub Inspector

They are usually in charge of armories. They are also a part of border patrolling with BSF. They make reports of incidents and report it to other senior officers.
